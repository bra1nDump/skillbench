### MARKET META SHIFTS

1. **Copilot → Agent:** The dominant narrative across every sub-category. Spellbook launched "Associate" (agent for lawyers). Ironclad shipped AI Agents. Notion shipped Custom Agents. The tools that ship agents first win the next wave.

2. **Gamma → Design Platform:** Gamma Imagine (Mar 2026) means Gamma is no longer a pitch deck tool — it's a design platform competing with Canva. The presentation sub-category competitive frame needs updating.

3. **Legal AI Consolidation:** Spellbook acquiring ~5 companies. Harvey at $11B. Ironclad at $200M+ ARR. Robin AI dead. The legal AI market is rapidly consolidating into 3-4 winners.

4. **Rows Sunset (May 31):** The #10 ranked tool is going away. Superhuman acquisition means the AI spreadsheet niche loses its most independent player. Energent.ai and Sourcetable/Pane are potential replacements.

5. **MCP Business Servers Fragmenting:** 47+ new business-related MCP servers found across registries, but most have <50 installs. The pattern is region-specific invoice/accounting servers (Hungarian, Czech, German, Costa Rican). No breakout business MCP tool yet.

6. **Enterprise Platform AI:** Stripe (MPP), QuickBooks (AI agents), Notion (Custom Agents), Microsoft (Work IQ MCP) are all shipping AI features into existing massive user bases. These are not "tools" in the SkillPack sense but they reshape what standalone tools need to offer.
